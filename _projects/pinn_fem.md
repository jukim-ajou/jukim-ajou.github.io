**Hybrid PINN-FEM-DeepONet Model for Real-Time Displacement Estimation Based on Inelastic Strain**

- **Period:** 2025 -- 2026
- **Role:** Principal Investigator
- **Funding:** National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF), Early Career Research Grant
- **Summary:** This project develops a hybrid computational framework that integrates Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN), Finite Element Methods (FEM), and DeepONet for real-time structural displacement estimation from inelastic strain measurements.
