**Seismic Performance Assessment of Nuclear Power Plant Structures**

Past major research projects in seismic and nuclear structural engineering:

- **Seismic capacity enhancement technology for NPPs** (KETEP, 2017--2022): Developed shear strength evaluation methods and seismic fragility analysis for squat RC walls in nuclear power plants.
- **Design-exceeding high-frequency earthquake assessment** (KETEP, 2017--2021): Evaluated seismic performance of NPP structures and equipment under high-frequency ground motions.
- **High-performance structural materials for NPP design** (KHNP, 2021--2022): Developed design element technologies using advanced structural materials.
