Welcome to the **ISDA Lab** (Intelligent Structural Design and Analysis Laboratory) at [Ajou University](https://www.ajou.ac.kr). Our research focuses on advancing structural engineering through the integration of mechanics-based theories and data-driven approaches.

## Research Interests

- **Flexural-Shear Design and Computational Analysis of RC Members** -- Development of shear strength models and computational methods for reinforced concrete structural elements
- **Probabilistic Seismic Performance and Fragility Assessment** -- Seismic fragility analysis and performance-based evaluation of reinforced concrete structures including nuclear power plant facilities
- **Physics-Informed Machine Learning for Structural Engineering** -- Hybrid PINN-FEM-DeepONet models and mechanics-based ML approaches for structural design and evaluation
- **Floor Impact Sound in Residential Buildings** -- Measurement, evaluation, and noise reduction system development for heavy-weight impact sound in apartment buildings
- **FRP-Reinforced Concrete Structures** -- Design and performance evaluation of concrete structures strengthened with fiber-reinforced polymer reinforcement
